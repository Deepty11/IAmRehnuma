About me

I am Rehnuma Reza. I am currently working as a Software Engineer in iOS development at Therap BD LTD one of the best software companies of Bangladesh since April, 2022. As a developer working here, I have been responsible for implementing new features for the existing iOS product, fixing bugs, solving problems, updating and improving libraries and dependencies in UIKit, applying different design and architectural patterns and maintaining the SOLID principle paradigm. Moreover, I have also gathered hands-on experience on SwiftUI 2.0, a new addition to iOS development.

Before Therap, I used to work as a Junior software engineer for about 9 months in another renowned research and development company named Brotecs Technologies LTD from late 2020 to early 2022. I started my development career working as an intern for about 6 months in the same company through getting selected from a coding contest. There, I was first introduced to iOS development and its scopes. In this company, my responsibility was to feature implementation of a newly developed app, management, code organization, bug fixing, product delivery to the beta users through app center, project management with JIRA and so on. Additionally, I was also responsible to communicate with the clients on a regular basis and get a grasp of the requirements. I experienced working with IoT platforms, monitoring with servers, ssh, telnet communication protocol and satellite communication technologies.

Before starting my career as a software developer, I had gathered a short period of teaching experience in Daffodil International University in the year 2020. There I have taught programming with C and Object-Oriented Programming with Java to certain classes. My responsibility there was to deliver lectures on problem solving and evaluate the students.
